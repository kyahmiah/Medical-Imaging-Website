Pork to Your Table

Working 'from field to fork' we produce fantastic tasting pork based around rare-breed Mangalitza and Tamworth pigs, reared in pockets of woodland around the farm.

Traditional bloodlines together with outdoor diets, naturally rich in advantageous omega-3 acids and antioxidants, produce superior flavour and nutritional value.

Sustainable Arable Small Farm: Big Ideas

Gothelney Farm is a 250 acre family run farm near Bridgwater, Somerset. The 224 acres of arable, medium-clay loam soils, are worked by Gerald and Fred. The farm operates a diverse five year rotation of a pulse break followed by two cereal crops, oil seed rape and a further cereal.

We are a family farm trying to prove small really can be beautiful through innovation and experimentation, embracing the latest ideas and technology. Our goal? Turn 200 acres into 300 acres, without extra land. Mindful of our past, responsible for our natural environment, a future dedicated to improvement.

Meetings and Catering The Stable Block

Unique, Characterful and Inspiring: the perfect space for a bespoke conference, training event or product launch - in fact any occasion.

The Stable Block is a superbly renovated building within the curtilage of Gothelney Hall, a beautiful Grade I listed farmhouse dating to 1400.
